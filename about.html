President & CEO

Arc. Sauban A. Obatade

MSC. MNIA. MNIM. FNIA

He is the President and the Chief Executive Officer of the consulting firm. He managed the foundation consortium (SHAABAN TREND CONSORTIUM) that gives the birth of the Limited Liability Company of SHAABAN TRENDS CONSULTS LIMITED for more than three decades.

Arc. Obatade holds credible credentials in architecture, regional planning, landscaping and interior works. He is a star membership of professional groups in Nigeria. He holds the prestigious membership of the College of Fellow of the Nigerian Institute of Architects and other professional bodies. He is a corporate and chartered member of the Nigerian Institute of Management (MNIM).

Arc. Obatade S.A. has to his credit several monumental structures from the designs, constructions consultancies and supervision works. He has participated and represented state governments, professional institutes on many project implementation, execution and policies implementation.
